Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) Hons in Computer Science

> Overview

The B.Sc. Hons in Computer Science program is a three-year undergraduate degree designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computer science and its applications.

> Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum educational qualification: 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board.
  • Minimum percentage requirement: 50% or as per the university's guidelines.
  • Entrance exam: Some universities may require students to qualify in an entrance examination.

> Course Details

The B.Sc. Hons Computer Science program typically includes a variety of core and elective courses. Some of the core courses with their credit values include:

  • Programming Using Python (4 credits)
  • Computer System Architecture (4 credits)
  • Mathematics for Computing (4 credits)
  • Object Oriented Programming with C++ (4 credits)
  • Discrete Mathematical Structures (4 credits)
  • Software Engineering (4 credits)

> Fee Structure

The fee structure may vary depending on the university or college. On average, the total fee for the entire program ranges from INR 40,000 to INR 1,00,000.

> Future Possibilities

Graduates with a B.Sc. Hons in Computer Science have a wide range of career options available to them, including:

  • Software Developer
  • Web Developer
  • Data Analyst
  • Database Administrator
  • Network Administrator
  • System Analyst
  • IT Consultant
  • Research Scientist

> Additionally, students can pursue higher education, such as M.Sc. in Computer Science or MBA in Information Technology, for further specialization.
